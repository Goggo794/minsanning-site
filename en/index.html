My Truth

Notes from a Christian Father

This is a book about stepping back.

About seeing the pattern behind what's happening to society, to the family, to manhood. About asking the larger questions again: God. Why we're here. How a family actually works — with the state and the mother at the center, or the way every generation before us understood it?

This is a testimony, not an opinion piece.

It's free. Download it, read it, share it. Or don't — that's not why it was written.
